About Us

Your Premier Partner in Plastic Raw Materials and Chemical Solutions

At OV Kunststoffe, we specialize in Business Development for Premium German brands. Our expertise lies in facilitating direct project supply tailored to meet specific requirements. With a focus on fostering strategic partnerships, we excel in negotiating licenses and joint ventures to enhance business growth and innovation. As a trusted intermediary, we bridge the gap between German technology providers and businesses seeking cutting-edge solutions. Our dedication to excellence ensures seamless integration of advanced German technology across diverse industries. In addition to our business development services, we are actively involved in trading petrochemical and polymer products. Our extensive portfolio includes trading in PVC, PP, HDPE, and PPR products, catering to the evolving needs of industries worldwide. At OV Kunststoffe, we are committed to delivering superior quality, reliability, and innovation. With a customer-centric approach, we strive to exceed expectations and drive sustainable growth for our partners and clients globally.
