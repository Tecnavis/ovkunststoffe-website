About Us

Your Premier Partner in Plastic Raw Materials and Chemical Solutions

At OV Kunststoffe, we specialize in Business Development for Premium German brands, including Ostendorf Kunststoffe. Our expertise lies in facilitating direct project supply tailored to meet specific requirements, such as soundproof pipes systems, Skolan safe solutions, HT safe materials, DWC pipes, and double-wall corrugator pipes. With a focus on fostering strategic partnerships, we excel in negotiating licenses and joint ventures to enhance business growth and innovation in the field of petrochemical products and polymer raw materials. As a trusted intermediary, we bridge the gap between German technology providers like Ostendorf Kunststoffe and businesses seeking cutting-edge solutions. Our dedication to excellence ensures seamless integration of advanced German technology, such as Ostendorf's products, across diverse industries. In addition to our business development services, we are actively involved in trading PVC, PP, HDPE, and PPR products,
